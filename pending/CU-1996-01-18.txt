DIGITAL COLORWRITER LSR 2000

LAST ORDER DATE CHANGED FOR VXT 2000 PRODUCTS
Taken from EISNER.

The last order and ship dates for the sale of VXT 2000 products in all 
non-European countries have changed. The last order date is February 15, 
1996; the last ship date is March 1, 1996. In Europe, both the last ship 
date and last order date have past.

For more information on the appropriate migration path, see the Product 
and Service Retirements section of the December 1995 issue of Customer 
Update.
