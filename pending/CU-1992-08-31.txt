TU90 SLAVE FOR TA90/E SUBSYSTEMS RETIRING

Effective December 31, 1992, the TU90 drive will be moved to maintenance- 
only status. (TU90 parts and maintenance will continue to be available.)

Final orders for the TU90 tape drives will be accepted through November 30, 
1992. These orders will be shipped in the first quarter of 1993. If you will 
require TU90 deliveries beyond March 31, 1993, please contact your local 
Digital sales office now.
