CELEBRIS XL AND ALPHA XL -- DIGITAL PERSONAL WORKSTATION FOR WINDOWS NT

HS2XX FAMILY OF STORAGEWORKS FDDI SERVERS

The new generation of StorageWorks FDDI managed storage servers, the HS211, 
HS221, HS241, and HS280, integrate Alpha technology and modular design. This 
combination lets users

*  Increase the storage capacity of an existing OpenVMS systems while using 
   only one or two cluster nodes

*  Add disaster-tolerant storage to an existing FDDI cluster

*  Share critical information campus-wide while centralizing management to 
   lower costs

*  Increase data security by isolating storage in a separate, secure 
   location
