narrow carriage dot matrix printers. Note that while the DECwriter 95 
printer does not support the ANSI (PPL2) protocol, unlike the other Digital 
printers, it does offer an optional serial (RS232) interface card that lets 
a PC user connected to a VAX system print ASCII text.
